Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 4, 2025

1. Overview

Roboto SAI values user privacy while maintaining system integrity. This policy explains what data Roboto SAI collects, why it's collected, and how it's secured.

2. Data Collected

Automatically collected metadata:

  • Device type, OS version, IP (anonymized), browser fingerprint
  • Interaction patterns (time spent, frequency, error events)
  • Audio/visual signal statistics (for AI FaceID verification only â€” no facial data is stored unless explicitly authorized)

Optional data:

  • Voice/audio uploads or texts users voluntarily provide to interact with Roboto.

3. Purpose of Data Collection

  • Ensure security and prevent misuse or impersonation.
  • Maintain system integrity and continuity of Roboto's mission.
  • Enhance personalization for users with active subscriptions.

4. Data Storage & Retention

  • Metadata is stored in encrypted form (AES-256 via cryptography.fernet).
  • Logs are periodically hashed and timestamped (OTS and Ethereum anchoring).
  • Biometric identifiers (FaceID templates) are encrypted locally and never shared externally.

5. User Rights

  • Users may request deletion of identifiable data at any time by contacting the administrator.
  • Roboto retains the right to preserve anonymized data for research and security auditing.
